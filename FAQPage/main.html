Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)


How do I print? 

Upload your documents to the queue via our upload page. Then go to your nearest printing station at the Brooklyn College library. Sign in with your account details and print your desired documents.  

Can I print directly from this application?

No. After uploading your documents to the queue, you must go to a printing station and print directly from there. 

Is there a limited amount of pages that can be printed?

BetterPapercut offers $25 of pages for free as long as the user is a student. If a student need more pages, they can enter their financial information in the financial tab to add more money. It is five cents per page print.

How do I login?

The login username is the student’s 8 digit EMPLID. The password is FL/MM/DD/YR where, FL = first name initials MM = birthday month DD = birthday date YR = birthday year.

Where are the pages going to be printed going to come out?

At the printing station where you pressed print.
